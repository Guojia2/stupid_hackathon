AQ-10 Screening Results

Scoring Information

Scoring: Only 1 point can be scored for each question.

Score 1 point for Definitely Agree or Slightly Agree on questions 1, 7, 8, and 10.

Score 1 point for Definitely Disagree or Slightly Disagree on questions 2, 3, 4, 5, 6, and 9.

If the individual scores 6 or above, consider referring them for a specialist diagnostic assessment.

Important: This screening tool is for informational purposes only and is not a diagnostic instrument. Only a qualified healthcare professional can make a diagnosis of autism spectrum condition.

This test is recommended in 'Autism: recognition, referral, diagnosis and management of adults on the autism spectrum' (NICE clinical guideline CG142).

If you have concerns about autism, please consult with a healthcare provider for a comprehensive evaluation.
